    Getting Warmed Up and in the Right Mindset!!

    1.

    Given \(\lim_{x\to\infty} f(x)=4\) and \(\lim_{x\to-\infty} f(x)=-2\text{,}\) what do you then know about any horizontal asymptotes of \(f(x)\text{?}\)
    🔗
    🔗

    2.

    Given the following information about \(g(x)\text{,}\) what do you know about the horizontal and vertical asymptotes?
    🔗
    \begin{equation*} \[ \lim_{x\to\infty} g(x)\to\infty \qquad \lim_{x\to-\infty} g(x)\to\infty \] \[ \lim_{x\to2^+} g(x)\to\infty \qquad \lim_{x\to2^-} g(x)\to-\infty \qquad \lim_{x\to5} g(x)\to\infty \] \end{equation*}

    1. Given \(f(x)=\frac{c}{x^n}\) where \(c\) is any real number and \(n>0\text{,}\) then \(\lim_{x\to\infty}f(x)=0\) and determine \(\lim_{x\to-\infty}f(x)\text{.}\)

      🔗
      🔗
